Homework 8: Geoprocessing Analysis

Madeline Scott

Question: What areas of Massachusetts are located more than two miles away from a fire station?

Geoprocessing tools used: Buffer, dissolve, difference

Method: I began by placing a two-mile buffer around each point in the dataset of points that represent fire stations in the state of Massachusetts. I then dissolved this buffer to create a single layer that represents areas in the state that fall within two miles in any direction of a fire station. Finally, to define the areas that fall further than two miles of a fire station, I used the difference tool to subtract the dissolved buffer from the polygon of Massachusetts.

Data sources: All data came from the Massachusetts government website. https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-layers

Layers used: Fire stations, counties
